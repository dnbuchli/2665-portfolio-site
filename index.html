JUST ME

Hello! My name is Dakotah Buchli, but my designer name is editionh. I am a small-town girl, born and raised just west of Lincoln, Nebraska. I have a passion for all things creative and thrive off of the opportunity to explore new things within the design world. I am a quick learner with the drive and determination to complete any task I put my mind to. I am most interested in the areas of print, typography, and photography, but am also finding a new love in web design. I value honesty and integrity in relationships and enjoy working with and around other people.
