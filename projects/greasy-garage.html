GREASY GARAGE

Application:

Logo and Style Guide (Identity, Print)

Greasy Garage Custom Motorcyles is an emerging company run by sole-proprietor, Duane Niedner. Greasy Garage needed a new identity that reflected the custom work the shop does and appealed to Generation X, those most likely looking to restore a motorcycle. By using grunge textures, I was able to call the grit and grime that goes along with mechanical work. The branded style guide lays out the guidelines for use of the identity and brand components.
