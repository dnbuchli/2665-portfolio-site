HYVEE FLORAL

Client:

HyVee (Floral Department)

Application:

Ad Campaign (Photography, Web, Print)

HyVee is a large grocery store chain that prides themselves on quality products and customer service. HyVee was looking for a way to appeal to a generation of men who might benefit of some assistance when ordering flowers, Generation X. Using a series of witty taglines will help draw an emotional and humorous connection to a man looking to purchase flowers. An additional online component offers a fun and simple way to order flowers and set dates for future reference.
