ALLIANCE FOR THE FUTURE OF AGRICULTURE IN NEBRASKA (A-FAN)

Application:

Promotional Booklet (Photography, Print)

A-FAN is a non-profit organization in Nebraska that helps boost Nebraskan's knowledge about popular farming topics. The company was looking for a booklet that specifically showed the benefits of farming livestock. I was able to appeal to an audience of males ranging between ages 18 and 28 by using sharp imagery and simplistic graphics to present the information in an engaging way.
