MAROON 5

Client:

Maroon 5

Application:

Tour Booklet (Print)

Maroon 5 is a pop music group born and bred in Los Angeles. With their last album, V, the group will embark on a world tour beginning in February 2015. They were looking for a booklet that reflects the group's personality and offered information regarding the tour. To appeal to the young Generation Y audience, I used bold patterns and colors partnered with experimental spreads to express the same energy Maroon 5 brings through in their music.
