PUP'S PAWS

Application:

Product Branding and Package Development (Package)

Founded in a small-town shoppe, Pup's Paws is a newly emerging dog food and supply company that was looking an identity and packaging system that better expressed their standards. They are looking to attract health concious Millenials by using a natural process to produce all items and food. Using recycled paper and a more personal, hand-written approach to type, I was able to create a cohesive system that reflects the small-town business and their quality goods.
