BEB'S JAM

Client:

Beb's Jam

Application:

Label Design (Package)

Beb's Jam was founded in 2010 by a couple in rural Maine. The business started out as a just a hobby and turned into a full-steam-ahead jam producing operation. They wanted to target couples between ages 25 and 50 who were conscious about their health and were looking for unique flavors of jam. I was able to create a cohesive line of labels that called attention to the rustic home-town of the founding couple.
