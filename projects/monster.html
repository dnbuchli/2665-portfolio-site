MOMMY'S LITTLE MONSTER

Application:

Logo and Identity System (Identity, Print)

Mommy's Little Monster is a specialty clothing line for children featured at a local boutique. Parents, specifically Millennials who are mothers, are the target audience. With the logo, the company wanted to attract those fashion concious mothers who are looking for more contemporary children's clothing. The logo and system reflects a child's personality in that it is playful and youthful, drawing an element of emotion.
