PINNACLE BANK ARENA

Client:

Pinnacle Bank Arena

Application:

Mobile Ticket Application (Mobile)

The Pinnacle Bank Arena is a new establishment in Lincoln, Nebraska that hosts sporting events and all types of entertainment. The Arena was looking for a way to better handle the process of selling tickets and a mobile application was the way to go. Generation Y, of whom 60% download mobile applications, is the primary audience for this app. I was able to approach the design in a way that reflected the architecture of the physical Area. I implemented similar geometric shapes, and center compositions to present the information clearly.
