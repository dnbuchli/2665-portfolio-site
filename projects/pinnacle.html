PINNACLE BANK ARENA

Application:

Mobile Ticket Application (Mobile)

The Pinnacle Bank Arena is a new establishment in Lincoln, Nebraska that hosts sporting events and all types of entertainment. The Arena was looking for a way to better handle the process of selling tickets and a mobile application was the way to go. Generation Y, of whom 60% download mobile applications, is the primary audience for this app.
